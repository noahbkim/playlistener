This step will give Playlistener limited access to your Spotify account. We'll request the following permissions:

  • Modify public playlists enables adding songs to one of your playlists.
  • Modify playback state enables adding songs to your queue.
  • Read playback state allows the bot to see the song and playlist you're currently listening to.
  • Read recently played: allows the bot to see the last couple songs you've listened to.
